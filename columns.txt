id : Identifier for each record or individual in the dataset.
Gender : Gender of the individual (Male/Female).
Age : Age in years.
Height : Height in meters.
Weight : Weight in kilograms.
family_history_with_overweight : Whether the individual has a family history of overweight (Yes/No).
FAVC : Frequent consumption of high-caloric food (Yes/No).
FCVC : Frequency of vegetable consumption – usually on a scale (e.g., 1 to 3).
NCP : Number of main meals the person eats per day.
CAEC : Consumption of food between meals – categories like: No, Sometimes, Frequently, Always.
SMOKE : Whether the person smokes (Yes/No).
CH2O : Daily water consumption – often measured on a scale (e.g., 1 = low, 3 = high).
SCC : Whether the person takes calorie supplements (Yes/No).
FAF : Physical activity frequency – hours of physical activity per week.
TUE : Time spent using technology (e.g., screen time) in hours.
CALC : Alcohol consumption – categories like: No, Sometimes, Frequently.
MTRANS : Means of transportation used – e.g., Walking, Bike, Public_Transportation, etc.
NObeyesdad : Target label – Obesity level class, such as: Insufficient_Weight, Normal_Weight, Overweight_Level_I, Obesity_Type_I, etc.
